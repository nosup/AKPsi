A Message from our President

Welcome to the Rho Psi chapter of Alpha Kappa Psi at Johns Hopkins University. Since 2001, our chapter has worked to develop principled business leaders. We are the business powerhouse of Johns Hopkins University. We work to bring together JHU’s highest achieving, business-minded students to foster a community of growth and success.

Our brotherhood is comprised of members from 20 different majors, from countries around the world, and from diverse backgrounds. Rho Psi brothers span the Hopkins community as leaders of several other student organizations, young entrepreneurs, achievers in academia, varsity collegiate athletes, and young professionals in the business world.

Alpha Kappa Psi is the oldest and largest professional business fraternity with more than 160,000 initiated members world-wide at 265 active chapters. Recognized as the premier developer of principled business leaders, AKPsi prides itself on sending driven and inspiring members into the professional sphere. Our chapter’s own alumni now currently work at companies such as JPMoragn, Bain & Co., Bridgewater Associates, McKinsey & Co., Pandora, Deloitte, Facebook, Accenture, and Goldman Sachs. Some have also gone on to become finalists at TechCrunch Disrupt, the world’s premier start up competition, while others have founded their own non-profit organizations.

We love to see our chapter grow and to add new members though recruitment. If you have any questions about Alpha Kappa Psi, do not hesitate to email our Executive Board at akpsijhu@gmail.com or check our Facebook Page ‘Alpha Kappa Psi - Johns Hopkins University.

Sarah Condon ’20 - President of Alpha Kappa Psi, Rho Psi Chapter
